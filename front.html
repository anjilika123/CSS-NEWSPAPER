OIL PRICE WATCH as of March 14, 2026

Fuel prices are expected to surge again next week, offering no relief to motorists. Diesel could increase by P19.30 to P22.30 per liter, while gasoline may rise by P14 to P17 per liter, as the ongoing crisis in the Middle East continues to disrupt shipments through the crucial Strait of Hormuz.

Oil prices are also being pushed higher as the Philippine peso weakened to a new record low of 59.735 against the US dollar on Friday, driven by concerns over a prolonged regional conflict and ongoing disruptions in the crucial global oil shipping route.
