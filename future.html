Our Community in 2035

Front Page Local News Community Opinion Future

In the future, our community may become a place where technology and education work together to improve everyday life. Smart classrooms could let students use digital tools and interactive systems that make learning more interesting and effective.

Transportation may also get better with improved road planning and eco-friendly vehicles. Public transport could become more organized and efficient, helping residents travel around the town more safely and quickly.

More youth programs and digital government services could also help shape the future of the community. Online platforms may let residents easily access barangay announcements, permits, and other information. By investing in education, technology, and youth development, the community can continue to grow and become a better place for everyone.
